### The Aegis Thesis
Humanity is gaining powerful intelligence but losing sovereignty over thought as systems centralize into opaque, state or corporate owned infrastructure. Aegis is architected as a guardian of agency: a system designed to ensure that as intelligence scales, autonomy scales with it. I build the infrastructure that protects the possibility space of independent minds, ensuring that the future of intelligence remains biological and artificial. Aegis does not decide the future. It ensures there is one.
